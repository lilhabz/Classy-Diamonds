About Classy Diamonds

For nearly 30 years, Classy Diamonds has been providing superior quality and customer service to its clientele. Ned is well-known for his meticulous custom design work. He goes above and beyond to ensure he is able to create the most detailed and flawless pieces of jewelry that his customers have been dreaming of. The dedication he has for his work is remarkable and never goes unnoticed. With a wide-range customer base, Ned serves customers all over the world including London, Quebec, and throughout Europe and Australia. He always looks forward to meeting new customers, building lifelong relationships, and turning dreams into reality.
